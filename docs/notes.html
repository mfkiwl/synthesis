Performance

  • Precision
  • Accuracy
  • NB: Performance extends beyond accuracy/precision
  • though used synonymously

Function

  • Can i get a position fix at all?

Required Performance

  • Can i get a position fix at a certain precision ?
  • In our case, what precision can i get on my position fix ?
  • How to test [required performance] this precision in the built environment is the meat of the synthesis
